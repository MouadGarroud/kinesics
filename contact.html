Common questions

Is Kinesics completely free? ▾

Yes! All lessons, letters, and signs are 100% free. No account is needed.

Which sign language do you teach? ▾

We currently focus on American Sign Language (ASL) for English fingerspelling and Arabic Sign Language for Arabic letters.

Can I suggest new signs? ▾

Absolutely! Email us with your suggestions and we'll review them for future content updates.

How long does it take to learn fingerspelling? ▾

Most beginners can learn the basics in a few hours of practice. Fluency comes with consistent daily practice over a few weeks.
